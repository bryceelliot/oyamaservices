Common questions

Good things to know.

How long does a pre-purchase inspection take?

A typical pre-purchase inspection takes 60–90 minutes in the shop. We usually deliver the written report the same day so you can make a decision quickly.

Do I need an appointment?

Yes — we work by appointment so every vehicle gets proper time on the lift and our full attention. Call (250) 862-6369 to book.

Do you work on all makes and models?

Yes. As an independent shop we service most domestic and import cars, trucks, and SUVs commonly driven in the Okanagan.

Can you inspect a vehicle I'm buying privately?

Absolutely — that's one of our most common jobs. Bring the vehicle (or send the seller) and we'll give you an independent, written report on its real condition.

Where are you located?

5325 Todd Rd, Lake Country, BC — minutes from Highway 97, between Kelowna and Vernon.

What do I bring?

The vehicle, the keys, and any service records you have. That's it.
