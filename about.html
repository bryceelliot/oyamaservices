About the shop

A local shop built on honest work.

We're a small, independent mechanic in Lake Country that does inspections and repairs the way they should be done — with the right tools, proper time on the lift, and a plain-English explanation of what we found.

Our approach

No dealership upsell. No guesswork.

Oyama Services is an independent shop, which means we don't sell you the car — we just tell you the truth about it. When you hand us the keys, you're paying for our expertise and a clear report, not for work that inflates the invoice.

We specialize in thorough vehicle inspections — mechanical, electrical, and structural — for Okanagan drivers buying used, evaluating a trade, or trying to figure out what's actually wrong with the vehicle they already own.

  • Independent — we answer to the vehicle owner, nobody else
  • Modern diagnostic equipment and proper lift time
  • Written reports with photos where it matters
  • Plain language, so you can act on what we found
